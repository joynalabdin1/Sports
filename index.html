Bangladesh Premier League, 2017

Malik, Tamim power Victorians to comprehensive win

Shoaib Malik and Al-Amin Hossain starred with the ball before Tamim Iqbal struck an unbeaten half-century to power Comilla Victorians to a commanding nine-wicket win over Khulna Titans in the 32nd match of the Bangladesh Premier League 2017 at the Zahur Ahmed Chowdhury Stadium in Chittagong on Tuesday (November 28). Shoaib Malik, who shared the new ball with Mahedi Hasan, left Titans in a spin as he dismissed three of the top five. Al-Amin then ran through the middle order to bowl Titans out for 111. In reply, Victorians rode on Tamim's unbeaten 64, along with useful knocks from Liton Das and Imrul Kayes, requiring less than 14 overs to reach the target.
